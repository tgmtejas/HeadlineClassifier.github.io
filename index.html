Headline Classifier

Principle of Artificial Intelligence Project
By Baktavatsalam, Sachin, Tejas

Goal

Design a text classifier to classify newspaper headlines into 4 groups
Business,health,entertainment,science

Evaluate the performance of the multi-class classifier

Methodology

Feature selection:

The vocabulary for the news classifier should carefully be selected as the performance is sensitive to this. For this project, the document frequency metric was used for feature selection.

Train the naive Bayes classifier:

The filter is multivariate Bernoulli and the conditional probability given the class for each feature in the dictionary is calculated. This is obtained using maximum likelihood estimate for Bernoulli random variable.

Evaluate:

Performance metric for multi-class classifier are used. They are based on the confusion matrix and involve and different metrics such as precision,accuracy and recall are computed.

Result & Conclusion

The classifier performs well despite the naive Bayes assumption.A classification accuracy of 90% was achieved.
The confusion matrix revealed the various misclassification and allowed us to compute the additional metrics.
